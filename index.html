Steven Paul Jobs

The man who changed the world by thinking different and played a huge role in creating the smartphone as we know it today.

Steve Jobs in 2008

A timeline of Steve's life :

  • 1955 - The adopted son of Paul Jobs.
  • 1972 - A college drop out.
  • 1972 - Jobs's first job.
  • 1974 - He becomes a buddhist.
  • 1976 - The Apple Computer Company was born.
  • 1983 - Hires John Sculley as the CEO of Apple.
  • 1984 - The Macintosh is introduced to the world in its famous Superbowl ad.
  • 1985 - Jobs is fired from Apple, a company that he founded.
  • 1985 - Jobs founded another company named NeXT Computer.
  • 1986 - Jobs purchased The Graphics Group that is widely known as Pixar today.
  • 1996 - Jobs returns to Apple.
  • 2004 - He is diagnosed with Pancreatic Cancer and takes his first medical leave.
  • 2005 - Jobs delivers his famous speech at Stanford.
  • 2007 - Jobs introduces the iPhone to the world.
  • 2009 - Jobs undergoes a liver transplant.
  • 2010 - Jobs introduces iPad.
  • June 2011 - Jobs gives his last keynote at Apple.
  • August 2006 - Tim Cook is appointed as his successor.
  • October 5, 2011 - The world goes Job-less.
